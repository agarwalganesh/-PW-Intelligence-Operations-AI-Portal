Core Strategic Pillars

Deep AI Integration

Leveraging proprietary tools like "AI Guru" (personal doubts bot) and "AI Grader" (for Mains evaluation) to resolve 90% of students doubts instantly and drive administrative cost reductions.

Omnichannel Vidyapeeth Network

Expanding offline footprint to 353 centres. Offline registrations grew 42% YoY in FY26, bringing high-structure local classrooms to Tier-2 & Tier-3 students at 60% lower costs than legacy offline hubs.

Ecosystem Diversification

Operating in 16+ exam verticals each clearing ₹10 Cr+ in collections annually (e.g. UPSC OnlyIAS, PW Skills, State Boards). This reduces dependency on the core online JEE/NEET engine.
